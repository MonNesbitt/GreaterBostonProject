The Project

The Eastern Massachusetts Life and Language Project is an oral histories project aimed at giving voice to the experiences of Bostonians. Eastern Massachusetts is one of the hardest hit US cities of the COVID-19 pandemic and we want to hear from you. We are interested in your stories as you navigate life under lock-down and during your transition out of it. We also recognize that African American communities are being hardest hit by this pandemic nationwide. We are especially interested in hearing about the lived experiences of African Americans living in and around the Greater Boston area.

There are two ways to participate in the project. You can either record a diary of yourself or sign up to be intereviewed via video conferencing by one of our students.
