The Project

The Dartmouth College Eastern Massachusetts Life and Language Project seeks to document the lived experiences of eastern Massachusetts natives from diverse backgrounds. We are especially interested in your experiences in the time of COVID-19.

Each phase of this project will focus on interviewing speakers from different ethnic groups. We are currently interviewing people from the eastern Massachusetts African American communtiy.

If you are African American and call eastern Massachusetts your hometown, we'd like to hear from you! Help us document the ways in which this pandemic has impacted language and life in your community.

We compensate each participant $12 for a 45-minute interview.
